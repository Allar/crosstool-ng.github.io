  * 20110911: [[http://sourceware.org/binutils/|binutils]] and [[http://sourceware.org/gdb/|gdb]] have recently renamed their tarballs due to a GPL compliance issue, and the old tarballs are now missing. The binutils guys were kind enough to provide //legacy// symlinks so that scripts using the old versions continue to work; the issue has been reported ([[http://sourceware.org/ml/gdb/2011-09/msg00002.html|here]] and [[http://sourceware.org/ml/gdb/2011-09/msg00030.html|here]]) to gdb as well. This means that crosstool-NG releasses so far choke when trying to download gdb. A fix to crosstool-NG to use the new versions is coming soon, but in the meantime, you'll have to manually [[ftp://ftp.gnu.org/pub/gnu/gdb/|download the gdb tarballs]], and rename it to the old names.
